Atty. Maureen P. Ruma

Managing Partner

Atty. Maureen P. Ruma is a licensed attorney and a member of the Integrated Bar of the Philippines since 2023. She graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Language and Literature from the University of the Philippines Baguio in 2018. Even before beginning her legal career, and continuing to the present, Atty. Ruma has served as an instructor at Isabela State University, where her expertise in language, communication, and research laid a strong foundation for her legal practice.

She specializes in civil, criminal, administrative, and property law, offering a comprehensive range of legal services, including consultancy, contract drafting and review, and representation for both individuals and corporate clients.

Committed to providing reliable, practical, and client-focused solutions, Atty. Ruma works closely with clients to understand their goals and craft effective strategies that address their legal needs with precision and confidence.

Atty. Mia Marinelle R. Labugen

Senior Partner

Atty. Mia Marinelle R. Labuguen is a licensed attorney and a member of the Integrated Bar of the Philippines since 2023. She holds a Bachelor of Arts in Social Sciences major in Economics and minor in Political Science from the University of the Philippines Baguio where she graduated in 2011.

Atty. Labuguen currently serves as the Municipal Government Department Head at the Local Government Unit of San Guillermo, Isabela. With over a decade of experience in public service, her extensive background in governance has sharpened her expertise in administrative law and government procurement law-- areas where she now focuses her legal practice.

Beyond her work in public administration, Atty. Labuguen is deeply engaged in community service particularly in advocating for the legal rights and welfare of women and children in her locality. Her dedication to both public service and the law makes her a valuable asset to our firm and to the communities she serves.
