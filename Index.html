Debt Collection

Unpaid debts can disrupt your business and cash flow. At Ruma Escalante Labuguen Law Office, we provide fast, effective, and legally compliant debt recovery services for individuals, businesses, and financial institutions in the Philippines.

Debt collection may be pursued through extrajudicial or judicial means, depending on the nature of the obligation and the debtor‚Äôs willingness to cooperate. Our office provides strategic legal assistance at every stage of the process‚Äîfrom initial demand to post-judgment enforcement.

Debt collection services include:

  • Pre-Litigation Collection
    • Demand Letters: Formal notices that state the obligation, interest due, and legal risks of non-payment.
    • Negotiation & Mediation: We seek settlement options to resolve matters quickly and amicably.
    • Debtor Tracing: We locate debtors and evaluate assets to assess collectability.
  • Court Action
    • Filing Collection Cases: When settlement fails, we initiate legal action, including:
      • Collection of Sum of Money
      • Breach of Contract
      • Replevin (for secured debts)
    • Judgment Enforcement: We use legal remedies like garnishment and levy to collect on the judgment.
  • Post-Judgment Support
    • Our commitment does not end with securing a favorable judgment. We take prompt and effective steps to enforce the court‚Äôs decision‚Äîcoordinating with sheriffs, banks, and enforcement agencies to implement asset seizure, wage garnishment, or account levies until full satisfaction is achieved.

We use strategic and legally compliant methods to maximize recovery while maintaining professional standards throughout the collection process.
